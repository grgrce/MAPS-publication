Listen to the future

Climate change is one of the biggest threats of human kind. In order to have a future for our children we need to replace fossil fuel plants for renewable energy like wind turbines. Though most people agree with this, they don’t want those energy towers in their backyard. All kind of arguments are presented to oppose the possible building of these turbines. One of the most used is the noise. It would be too loud, it would be dreadfull they say. Even life threatening in some cases. I took the proof of the pudding, and went to several wind turbines. How annoying is it really?
